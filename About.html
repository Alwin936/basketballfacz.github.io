About

Basketball Facz is a Passion Project by a 6th Grader named Alwin Thomas. He came into this project knowning HTML, CSS, a decent amount of Javascript, and relative pinches of Python. He also had an air of thought that this project wouldn't be very hard, maybe on the slightly lower end. He was very VERY mistaken. To complete only phase one of this website, he spent a total of more than 33 hours. His advice to anyone who would like to develop their own website is that know that you will have to give a good amount of effort to get a decent looking website done. Yet, at the same time, never be afraid to take on a challenge.
